> 4. Blood Ammonia Level

> <mark>Clinical Features</mark>
>
> 1. Charcot's Triad: Abdominal pain: Non-colicy RUQ pain to right shoulder; Jaundice; Fever
>
> 2. Pentad: + Hypotension + Confusion/Dropping GCS
>
> 3. Biliary Obstruction: Dark Urine and Pale Stool

> <mark>Investigations</mark>
>
> 1. Bloods: Routine (Raised WCC, Raised Bilirubin, Raised ALP, Raised GGT, Raised ALT)
>
> 2. Blood Cultures (50% positive)
>
> 3. Imaging: Abdominal US (Enlarged gallbladder with stones and thick wall; stones in CBD suggested if duct diameter >5 mm) ASAP 1Ô∏è‚É£
>
> 4. Imaging: Abdominal CT (see obstruction; measure common bile duct; excludes pancreatic carcinoma etc) ‚≠êÔ∏è
>
> 5. Imaging: MRCP (but ofc challenging with pacemaker) -> ERCP

> <mark>Management</mark>
>
> 1. Supportive: NBM, NG if vomitting; IVF
>
> 2. IV Abx: Tazocin 4.5g TDS for 7 days
